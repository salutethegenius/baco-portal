## Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge recommended)
2. Navigate to the BACO Portal URL (provided by your administrator)
3. The portal will load the login/registration page

### Browser Requirements

- **Recommended:** Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
- **JavaScript:** Must be enabled
- **Cookies:** Must be enabled for session management
- **Screen Resolution:** Minimum 1024x768 for optimal experience

## Member Onboarding Guide

### Step 1: Registration

**For New Members:**

1. On the login page, click the **"Sign Up"** tab (or "Register" button)
2. Fill in the registration form with the following information:
   - **Email Address** - Use your professional email address
   - **Password** - Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Confirm Password** - Re-enter your password
   - **First Name** - Your legal first name
   - **Last Name** - Your legal last name

3. Click **"Create Account"** or **"Register"**

4. You will see a success message: "Registration Successful! Welcome to BACO! Your membership is pending approval."

5. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

**Important Notes:**
- Your account will be created with **"Pending"** membership status
- You must wait for admin approval before your membership becomes active
- You will receive a confirmation email at the address you provided

### Step 2: First Login

**If you already have an account:**

1. On the login page, enter your **Email Address** and **Password**
2. Click **"Sign In"** or **"Log In"**
3. You will be redirected to your dashboard

**Forgot Password?**
1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** link on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link in the email to reset your password
5. Enter your new password twice
6. Log in with your new password

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

**After logging in for the first time:**

1. Click on **"Profile"** in the top navigation menu
2. Fill in or update the following information:

   **Personal Information:**
   - Phone Number
   - Address (Home Address)
   - Business Address (if applicable)
   - Date of Birth
   - Place of Birth
   - Nationality

   **Professional Information:**
   - Position/Job Title
   - Company Name
   - Current Employer
   - Years of Experience
   - Qualification
   - Institution
   - Graduation Year

   **Membership Information:**
   - Membership Type (Professional, Associate, etc.)
   - Bahamas Resident (Yes/No)
   - Years in Bahamas (if applicable)
   - Existing Member (if you were a member before)
   - Membership Number (if applicable)

3. Click **"Save Changes"** or **"Update Profile"** button
4. You will see a success message confirming your profile was updated

### Step 4: Upload Required Documents

**Documents you may need to upload:**

1. Navigate to **"Documents"** in the top navigation menu
2. Click the **"Upload Document"** button
3. In the upload dialog:
   - Select **Document Category** from the dropdown:
     - Professional Certificate
     - Identification (ID)
     - Payment Receipt
     - Other
   - Enter a **Document Name** (optional, but recommended)
   - Click the upload area to select your file
   - Supported file types: PDF, DOC, DOCX (up to 10MB)
   - Click **"Upload Document"** button

4. Your document will be uploaded and marked as **"Pending"** status
5. Wait for admin approval (you'll receive an email notification when approved/rejected)

**Document Statuses:**
- **Pending** (Yellow) - Awaiting admin review
- **Approved** (Green) - Document verified and accepted
- **Rejected** (Red) - Document needs correction or resubmission

**Viewing Your Documents:**
- All your documents are listed on the Documents page
- Click **"Download"** to view/download any approved document
- Rejected documents will show a message explaining why they were rejected

### Step 5: Membership Payment

**Understanding Payment Status:**

- **Pending Members:** Payment due date shows as **February 15th, 2025** until payment is received
- **Active Members:** Payment due date shows the next **February 15th** based on current date

**To Pay Membership Fee:**

1. Navigate to your **Dashboard**
2. Check the **"Next Payment Due"** card in the stats section
3. If payment is due, you can:
   - Click on payment-related links if available
   - Contact admin through the messaging system for payment instructions
   - Admin will process your payment and update your status

**Payment Methods:**
- Credit/Debit Card (via Stripe)
- Bank Transfer
- Cheque
- Other methods as arranged with admin

**After Payment:**
- Admin will update your payment status
- Your membership status will change from "Pending" to "Active"
- You will receive a confirmation email

## Member Portal Features

### Dashboard

Your dashboard is the main page you see after logging in. It shows:

**Stats Cards (Top Row):**
1. **Membership Status** - Shows "Active" or "Pending"
2. **Next Payment Due** - Shows the payment due date (February 15th yearly)
3. **Documents** - Click to navigate to Documents page
4. **Unread Messages** - Number of unread messages from admin

**Main Content Area:**

**Upcoming Events:**
- Displays the next 3 upcoming events
- Shows event title, date, location, and price
- Click **"View All Events"** to see all events
- Click on an event card to see details and register

**Recent Activity:**
- Shows your last 10 account activities:
  - Event registrations
  - Document uploads
  - Payments made
  - Messages received
- Activities are sorted by date (most recent first)

**Sidebar:**

**My Documents:**
- Quick summary of your documents
- Shows counts: Approved, Pending, Rejected
- Lists your 3 most recent documents
- Click to view full Documents page

**Recent Messages:**
- Shows your 3 most recent messages from admin
- Displays subject and preview
- Click to view full Messages page

**Quick Actions:**
- **Message Admin** - Opens messaging page to contact administrators
- **View Events** - Navigate to Events page
- **Upload Document** - Navigate to Documents page

### Documents Page

**Managing Documents:**

**Document Categories:**
- Professional Certificate
- Identification

**Profile Sections:**

**Changing Password:**

## Admin Access Guide

### Accessing the Admin Panel

**Prerequisites:**
- You must have an admin account
- Admin accounts are created by system administrators
- Contact the system administrator if you need admin access

**Login Process:**

1. Navigate to the BACO Portal login page
2. Enter your **admin email address** and **password**
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. After login, you'll see an **"Admin"** badge in the top navigation
5. Click the **"Admin"** badge to access the admin panel

**Default Admin Credentials:**
- Email: `admin@baco.com` (or as configured)
- Password: Contact system administrator for initial password

**Important Security Notes:**
- Change your password immediately after first login
- Use a strong, unique password
- Never share your admin credentials
- Log out when finished (especially on shared computers)

### Admin Panel Overview

The admin panel has **5 main tabs:**

1. **Members** - Manage member accounts
2. **Events** - Create and manage events
3. **Documents** - Review and verify member documents
4. **Messages** - Manage member communications
5. **Stats** - View system statistics (if available)

## Admin Panel Features

### Members Tab

**Viewing All Members:**

1. Click **"Admin"** in navigation, then **"Members"** tab
2. You'll see a table listing all members with:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Membership Status (Active/Pending)
   - Membership Type
   - Join Date
   - Actions

**Member Statistics:**
- Total Members count
- Active Members count
- Pending Documents count

**Searching and Filtering:**
- Use the search box to find members by name or email
- Filter by membership status if filters are available

**Viewing Member Details:**

1. Click on a member's name or row
2. View complete member profile including:
   - Personal information
   - Professional information
   - Membership details
   - Documents uploaded
   - Payment history
   - Event registrations

**Editing Member Information:**

1. Find the member in the members table
2. Click **"Edit"** or the member's name
3. Update any fields:
   - Membership Status (Pending → Active)
   - Membership Type
   - Annual Fee
   - Next Payment Date
   - Any other profile fields
4. Click **"Save Changes"** or **"Update Member"**
5. Changes are saved immediately

**Activating a Member:**

1. Find the member with "Pending" status
2. Click **"Edit"** or open member details
3. Change **Membership Status** from "Pending" to "Active"
4. Update **Next Payment Date** if needed (default: February 15th of current/next year)
5. Click **"Save Changes"**
6. Member will receive email notification (if configured)

**Deleting a Member:**

⚠️ **WARNING:** This action cannot be undone and will delete all associated data.

1. Find the member you want to delete
2. Click **"Delete"** button (usually red/destructive)
3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog
4. All member data will be permanently deleted:
   - Profile information
   - Documents
   - Messages
   - Event registrations
   - Payment records

**Adding a New Member Manually:**

1. Click **"Add Member"** or **"Create Member"** button
2. Fill in required information:
   - Email (must be unique)
   - Password (temporary, member should change)
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Membership Type
   - Membership Status
3. Click **"Create Member"**
4. Member will receive login credentials via email (if configured)

### Events Tab

**Viewing All Events:**

1. Click **"Events"** tab in admin panel
2. See a list of all events with:
   - Event Title
   - Start Date
   - End Date
   - Location
   - Price
   - Max Attendees
   - Current Attendees
   - Registration Status

**Creating a New Event:**

1. Click **"Create Event"** or **"New Event"** button
2. Fill in the event form:

   **Required Fields:**
   - **Title** - Event name (e.g., "BACO Annual Conference 2025")
   - **Description** - Detailed event description
   - **Start Date** - When the event begins
   - **End Date** - When the event ends
   - **Location** - Event venue/address
   - **Price** - Registration fee (enter 0 for free events)
   - **Max Attendees** - Maximum number of registrations allowed

   **Optional Fields:**
   - **Registration Closed** - Check to close registration
   - **Flyer Image** - Upload event flyer/image

3. Click **"Create Event"** or **"Save Event"**
4. Event will appear in the events list
5. Members can now see and register for the event

**Editing an Event:**

1. Find the event in the events list
2. Click **"Edit"** button
3. Update any fields
4. Click **"Update Event"** or **"Save Changes"**
5. Changes are reflected immediately

**Deleting an Event:**

⚠️ **WARNING:** Only delete if there are no registrations, or all registrations will be deleted.

1. Find the event
2. Click **"Delete"** button
3. Confirm deletion
4. If event has registrations, you'll see an error - delete registrations first

**Viewing Event Registrations:**

1. Find the event in the events list
2. Click **"View Registrations"** button
3. See a table of all registrations with:
   - Registrant Name
   - Email
   - Registration Type
   - Payment Status
   - Payment Method
   - Registration Date
   - Actions

**Managing Event Registrations:**

1. Open **"View Registrations"** for an event
2. For each registration, you can:
   - **View Details** - See full registration information
   - **Edit Registration** - Update registration details (see below)
   - **Export CSV** - Download registrations as CSV file

1. Click **"Manage"** or **"Edit"** button on a registration
2. Update the following fields:

   **Membership Information:**
   - **Membership Type** - Set whether registrant is Member or Non-Member
   - **Original Tier** - Shows original registration tier (read-only)

   **Payment Information:**
   - **Mark as Paid** - Toggle to mark payment as received

   - **CROS** - Reference number for CROS tracking
   - **Admin Notes** - Private notes visible only to admins

**Exporting Event Registrations:**

1. Open **"View Registrations"** for an event
2. Click **"Export CSV"** button
3. CSV file will download with all registration data
4. File is named: `[Event Title]_registrations_[Date].csv`

**Closing Event Registration:**

1. Edit the event
2. Check the **"Registration Closed"** checkbox
3. Save the event
4. Members will no longer be able to register

## Common Workflows

### Workflow 1: Onboarding a New Member

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Member Registers:**
   - Member creates account on portal
   - Account created with "Pending" status
   - Member receives confirmation email

2. **Admin Reviews Registration:**
   - Admin logs into admin panel
   - Goes to Members tab
   - Finds new member (status: Pending)
   - Reviews member information

3. **Member Uploads Documents:**
   - Member uploads required documents
   - Documents appear in Documents tab with "Pending" status

4. **Admin Reviews Documents:**
   - Admin goes to Documents tab
   - Reviews each document
   - Approves or rejects documents
   - Adds notes if needed

5. **Admin Activates Member:**
   - Admin goes back to Members tab
   - Edits member profile
   - Changes status from "Pending" to "Active"
   - Sets membership type and fee
   - Saves changes

6. **Member Receives Notification:**
   - Member receives email (if configured)
   - Member can now access all features
   - Payment due date is set

7. **Member Makes Payment:**
   - Member pays membership fee
   - Admin updates payment status
   - Member's account is fully active

### Workflow 2: Creating and Managing an Event

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Create Event:**
   - Admin goes to Events tab
   - Clicks "Create Event"
   - Fills in all event details
   - Sets price and max attendees
   - Saves event

2. **Event Goes Live:**
   - Event appears on Events page
   - Members can see and register
   - Registration is open

3. **Monitor Registrations:**
   - Admin periodically checks "View Registrations"
   - Sees who has registered
   - Tracks payment status

4. **Process Payments:**
   - As payments come in, admin updates registration:
     - Marks as "Paid"
     - Records payment method
     - Adds CROS reference if applicable
     - Adds admin notes

5. **Close Registration:**
   - When event is full or deadline approaches
   - Admin edits event
   - Checks "Registration Closed"
   - Saves event

6. **Export Registration List:**
   - Before event, admin exports CSV
   - Uses list for check-in, name tags, etc.

7. **Post-Event:**
   - Admin can archive or delete event
   - Keep registration data for records

### Workflow 3: Document Verification Process

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Member Uploads Document:**
   - Member goes to Documents page
   - Uploads document with category
   - Document status: Pending

2. **Admin Notification:**
   - Admin sees pending document in Documents tab
   - Document is highlighted or marked

3. **Admin Reviews:**
   - Admin clicks to view/download document
   - Reviews document for:
     - Completeness
     - Legibility
     - Authenticity
     - Compliance with requirements

4. **Admin Decision:**
   - **If Approved:**
     - Click "Approve" button
     - Document status: Approved
     - Member receives email notification
   
   - **If Rejected:**
     - Click "Reject" button
     - Add rejection reason (optional)
     - Document status: Rejected
     - Member receives email with reason

5. **Member Action (if rejected):**
   - Member sees rejection notice
   - Member uploads corrected document
   - Process repeats from step 1

### Workflow 4: Responding to Member Messages

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Member Sends Message:**
   - Member composes message to admin
   - Message appears in admin's Messages tab

2. **Admin Sees Notification:**
   - Unread count badge shows new messages
   - Conversation appears in Messages tab

3. **Admin Opens Conversation:**
   - Clicks on conversation
   - Reads member's message
   - Reviews conversation history if applicable

4. **Admin Responds:**
   - Types reply in text area
   - Clicks "Send Reply"
   - Reply appears in conversation

5. **Member Receives Response:**
   - Member sees reply in Messages page
   - Unread indicator shows new message
   - Conversation continues as needed

## Troubleshooting

### Member Issues

**Problem: Can't Log In**

**Solutions:**
1. Check that you're using the correct email address
2. Verify your password is correct (check for caps lock)
3. Click "Forgot Password?" to reset your password
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser
6. Contact admin if problem persists

**Problem: Forgot Password**

**Solutions:**
1. Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email (including spam folder)
4. Click the reset link in the email
5. Enter new password twice
6. Log in with new password
7. If email doesn't arrive, contact admin

**Problem: Can't Upload Document**

**Solutions:**
1. Check file size (must be under 10MB)
2. Check file type (PDF, DOC, DOCX only)
3. Make sure you're logged in
4. Try a different browser
5. Clear browser cache
6. Contact admin if problem persists

**Problem: Document Upload Hangs at 100%**

**Solutions:**
1. Wait a few moments - upload may still be processing
2. Close the upload dialog and check Documents page
3. If document appears, the upload was successful
4. If not, try uploading again
5. Contact admin if issue continues

**Problem: Can't See My Documents**

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you're on the Documents page
2. Refresh the page
3. Check if documents are pending approval
4. Contact admin if documents should be visible

**Problem: Payment Status Not Updated**

**Solutions:**
1. Payment processing may take time
2. Check your email for payment confirmation
3. Contact admin to verify payment was received
4. Admin will update your status manually

**Problem: Can't Register for Event**

**Solutions:**
1. Check if registration is closed
2. Verify event hasn't reached max attendees
3. Make sure you're logged in
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Contact admin if problem persists

### Admin Issues

**Problem: Can't Access Admin Panel**

**Solutions:**
1. Verify you're logged in with an admin account
2. Check that your account has `isAdmin: true`
3. Contact system administrator
4. Try logging out and back in
5. Clear browser cache and cookies

**Problem: Can't Edit Member**

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you're in the Members tab
2. Click "Edit" button on the member row
3. Verify you have admin permissions
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Contact system administrator if issue persists

**Problem: Event Not Showing to Members**

**Solutions:**
1. Check that event is saved
2. Verify registration is not closed
3. Check event dates (past events may not show)
4. Make sure event is published/active
5. Ask a member to refresh their Events page

**Problem: Can't Approve Document**

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you're in Documents tab
2. Click "Approve" button
3. Verify document is in "Pending" status
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Contact system administrator if button doesn't work

**Problem: Export CSV Not Working**

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you have registrations to export
2. Try a different browser
3. Check browser download settings
4. Disable pop-up blockers
5. Try right-click and "Save As" if download starts

**Problem: Messages Not Sending**

**Solutions:**
1. Check that you've selected a member
2. Verify subject and message are filled
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Check browser console for errors
5. Contact system administrator

### General Issues

**Problem: Page Not Loading**

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Check if the portal is under maintenance
6. Contact system administrator

**Problem: Slow Performance**

**Solutions:**
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check your internet speed
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact system administrator if consistently slow

**Problem: Error Messages**

**Solutions:**
1. Read the error message carefully
2. Note what action you were performing
3. Try the action again
4. If error persists, note the exact error message
5. Contact system administrator with error details

## Best Practices

### For Members

**Account Security:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared computers
- Change your password regularly
- Enable two-factor authentication if available

**Document Upload:**
- Upload clear, legible documents
- Use PDF format when possible
- Name documents descriptively
- Upload documents as soon as possible
- Keep copies of uploaded documents

**Communication:**
- Use clear, professional language in messages
- Include relevant details in your messages
- Check messages regularly
- Respond promptly to admin inquiries

**Profile Management:**
- Keep your profile information up to date
- Update contact information if it changes
- Complete all profile fields
- Upload required documents promptly

**Event Registration:**
- Register early for popular events
- Complete payment promptly
- Read event details carefully
- Contact admin if you have questions

### For Admins

**Member Management:**
- Review new registrations promptly
- Verify member information before activating
- Keep member records up to date
- Document any special circumstances in notes

**Document Verification:**
- Review documents within 2-3 business days
- Provide clear feedback when rejecting documents
- Keep verification standards consistent
- Document any special cases

**Event Management:**
- Create events well in advance
- Include all necessary details
- Set realistic attendee limits
- Monitor registrations regularly
- Export registration lists before events

**Communication:**

**Data Management:**
- Export important data regularly

### Data Protection & Privacy (Admins)

- Only access member information when you have a clear, legitimate reason related to your BACO role.

- Immediately report any suspected data breach (for example, accidental disclosure or unauthorised access) to the BACO Data Protection contact.

## FAQs

### Member FAQs

**Q: How do I know if my membership is active?**
A: Check your Dashboard - the "Membership Status" card will show "Active" instead of "Pending". You'll also receive an email notification when your membership is activated.

**Q: When is my membership payment due?**
A: Membership payments are due annually on **February 15th**. The exact date is shown on your Dashboard in the "Next Payment Due" card.

**Q: What documents do I need to upload?**
A: Required documents vary by membership type. Common documents include:
- Professional certificates
- Identification (ID)
- Payment receipts
- Other documents as requested by admin

**Q: How long does document approval take?**
A: Document approval typically takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive an email notification when your document is approved or rejected.

**Q: Can I edit my profile after registration?**
A: Yes! Go to the Profile page and update any information. Click "Save Changes" to update your profile.

**Q: How do I register for an event?**
A: Go to the Events page, click on an event, and click "Register for Event". Fill in the registration form and submit.

**Q: Can I cancel my event registration?**
A: Contact admin through the Messages system to request cancellation. Refund policies vary by event.

**Q: How do I contact admin?**
A: Use the Messages page to send a message to administrators. Click "Compose Message" or "Message Admin" from your Dashboard.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the email you receive.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Contact admin through Messages to request an email change. Email addresses are unique and require admin assistance to change.

### Admin FAQs

**Q: How do I create an admin account?**
A: Admin accounts must be created by a system administrator or through the database directly. Contact the system administrator for assistance.

**Q: How do I activate a new member?**
A: Go to Members tab, find the member, click Edit, change Membership Status from "Pending" to "Active", and save changes.

**Q: How do I process a payment?**
A: After receiving payment, go to the member's profile or event registration, mark payment as received, select payment method, and save.

**Q: Can I delete a member?**
A: Yes, but this permanently deletes all their data. Use with caution. Go to Members tab, find the member, and click Delete.

**Q: How do I export event registrations?**
A: Go to Events tab, click "View Registrations" for an event, then click "Export CSV" button.

**Q: What should I do if a document is unclear?**
A: Reject the document and provide clear feedback in the rejection reason. The member can then upload a clearer version.

**Q: How do I close event registration?**
A: Edit the event and check the "Registration Closed" checkbox. Save the event.

**Q: Can I edit event registrations?**
A: Yes! Click "Manage" on any registration to edit membership type, payment status, payment method, CROS reference, and admin notes.

**Q: How do I send a message to all members?**
A: Currently, you need to send individual messages. Bulk messaging may be available in future updates.

**Q: What if a member can't log in?**
A: Help them reset their password using the "Forgot Password" feature. If that doesn't work, you may need to reset their password manually in the database (contact system administrator).

**Q: How do I see all pending documents?**
A: Go to Documents tab and filter by "Pending" status, or look for documents marked with yellow/pending badges.

**Q: Can I change a member's email address?**
A: Email addresses are unique identifiers. Contact the system administrator to change a member's email address, as this requires database-level changes.
